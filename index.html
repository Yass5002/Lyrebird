Quick Start Guide

How to Use

  1. 1. Upload 5-15 seconds of clean, clear speech

Best Practices

  • Use high-quality audio (no background noise)
  • Single speaker only in reference
  • Clear pronunciation required
  • Optimal reference length: 5-15 seconds
